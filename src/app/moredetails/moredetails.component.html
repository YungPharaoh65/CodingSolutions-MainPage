About Coding Solutions


At Coding Solutions, our mission is to provide accessible and
practical web development training to individuals who are eager to learn.
We specialize in teaching the fundamentals
of creating simple yet functional websites,
ensuring that our learners gain a strong foundation in web development
from the ground up.
Our courses are designed to be beginner-friendly,
allowing those with no prior experience to quickly get started on their journey into the world of coding.





We cover a wide range of essential programming languages and technologies that are commonly used in the industry today.
From front-end languages like
HTML, CSS, and JavaScript
to back-end technologies like PHP, we aim to give our students a comprehensive understanding of how websites are built and maintained.
Our hands-on approach ensures that learners don’t just acquire theoretical knowledge but also develop practical skills they can apply immediately.

We offer a variety of classes to suit different learning preferences and schedules.
For those looking for personalized attention,
we provide individual training sessions, both in-person and virtually, allowing learners to progress at their own pace
with direct guidance from our instructors.
Our virtual classes are designed to be flexible and accessible from anywhere, giving you the freedom to learn in your own environment.


In addition to individual sessions, we also offer group classes three times a year.
These group classes foster collaboration, where students can work together, share ideas, and support each other's learning journeys.
Our class packages vary, depending on the level of commitment and the depth of learning you're looking for.
Whether you want a short-term course or an in-depth learning experience, we have options to suit your needs.


At Coding Solutions, we understand that starting out in web development can feel overwhelming. That’s why we offer personalized guidance
to help beginners navigate their way through the complexities of coding.
Whether you're looking to build your first website or enhance your existing skills, our resources and experienced instructors
are here to provide the support you need. We also encourage learners to explore
more advanced topics as they progress,
empowering them to continue growing and adapting to the ever-evolving field of web development.
